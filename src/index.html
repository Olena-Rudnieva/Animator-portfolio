About me

Hello!

My name’s Veronika and I’m a Ukrainian animation student in Paris. I’m seeking opportunities in the fields of animation, concept art, and game art.

I have 3 years of experience in illustration and currently I’m a student at LISAA Animation & Jeu Video, where I’ve been learning 2D animation techniques, character design, storyboarding, and visual development. I’ve been actively looking for opportunities to apply my skills and gain practical experience. Working on various collaborative projects, both in a team setting and individually, have allowed me to develop effective communication, problem-solving, and of course introduced me to the industry-standard software such as Adobe Creative Suite, Toon Boom Harmony, Autodesk Maya and Blender 3D.

In conclusion, my passion for animation, coupled with my illustration background, and practical experience make me a good team member. I’m open to all the possibilities, including internships and remote work. Thank you for checking my portfolio. I look forward to bring your creative visions to life!
